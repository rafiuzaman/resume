Welcome to Dr. Rafi U Zaman's Website

Associate Professor, IT Department, Muffakham Jah College of Engineering and Technology, Hyderabad

I have over 17 years of teaching experience at undergraduate and post graduate levels. I have presented and published numerous technical papers in reputed International Conferences and Journals. I obtained MCA and M. Tech (CSE) degrees (both with distinction) from Osmania University in 2002 and 2009 respectively. I was awarded the Doctor of Philosophy degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Osmania University in 2018, for the thesis titled “Efficient and Seamless integration of Mobile Ad Hoc Networks and Wired Networks”. Additionally, I have supervised many post graduate students in their major projects. My current research interests are: Application of soft computing methods in Integrated Internet MANET and Trust management in Integrated Internet MANET. I am a Life Member of Computer Society of India (CSI).
